NORTHEAST
MAJESTIC PEAKS

The sheer scale of the mountains here defies logic. Cascading, vividly green rice terraces cut into the sides of the Earth, stretching as far as the eye can see. The indigenous culture remains deeply woven into the landscape itself, where every winding turn presents a humbling reminder of our smallness amidst the grand theater of nature.

THE STRANGE BEAUTY IN THE HIGHLANDS

The waterfall primarily originates from a hydroelectric dam, as the government encourages further dam development in the highlands.

The villages in the highlands draw large numbers of tourists, who come to witness the majestic green rice terraces and the houses nestled within them.

The houses and villages are built on rugged, steep, and uneven highland terrain, adapting to the natural landscape.
