HO CHI MINH
AT NIGHT

Night breathes life into Ho Chi Minh City, where glowing lights stretch across the skyline and streets, forming a vibrant pattern of constant motion. Even after dark, the city pulses with energy,motorbikes flow like streams, signs reflect on the roads, and everything moves in a fast, continuous rhythm. My hometown feels dense, alive, and endlessly energetic, as if time itself rushes through the night.
