VINH HY
THE HIDDEN BAY

A fiercely guarded secret of the coastline. The dramatic, jagged cliffs plunge violently into waters so crystal clear that they mirror the sky perfectly. Exploring Vinh Hy feels like discovering a pristine, untouched slice of the Earth, where the mountains seamlessly greet the deep blue ocean, isolated from modern noise.

PEACEFUL BEACH

As a developing beach city, it creates a strange yet fascinating feeling, with hopes that this place will grow into a more widely known and beautiful beach and bay.

Immersing ourselves in the crystal-blue beach with warm summer waters creates a truly wonderful travel experience.

Climbing into a boat or canoe to float around the island and bay, taking in jagged cliffs and extraordinary rock formations.

Unlike Vung Tau Beach’s energetic waves, this new beach is shaped by soft, peaceful tides that move gently across the ocean.

Beyond the beach’s beauty, blooming flowers create an endless garden that sways gently in the wind, forming a picturesque scene.
