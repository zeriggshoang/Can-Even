THE HIDDEN BAY

A fiercely guarded secret of the coastline. The dramatic, jagged cliffs plunge violently into waters so crystal clear that they mirror the sky perfectly. Exploring Vinh Hy feels like discovering a pristine, untouched slice of the Earth, where the mountains seamlessly greet the deep blue ocean, isolated from modern noise.
