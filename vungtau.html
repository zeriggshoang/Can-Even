VUNG TAU
COASTAL BREEZE

The gentle breeze coming off the coast carries the salty, refreshing kiss of the South China Sea. As the sun begins to set, the entire shoreline is painted in brilliant strokes of amber, violet, and soft pink. Vung Tau offers an unadulterated escape from the restless city, where the rhythmic crashing of waves against rocky cliffs creates a natural symphony.
