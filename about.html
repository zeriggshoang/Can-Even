Photgrapher and Videographer

Hoang Ha (Zeriggs)

I am currently a final-year Digital Media student at RMIT University Vietnam. My journey began in Grade 11, laying the foundation to explore digital media and discover my strengths in photography, videography, and post-production.

Through various photography and videography projects, I developed a strong interest in cinematic composition, camera angles, and visual effects that enhance the emotional depth of a scene.

For me, every photograph and footage should communicate a clear emotion and meaningful story through visual language. The moments and environments I capture often reflect personal experiences.

Color also plays an important role in my creative process, as it strongly affects mood and emotional perception. Through color grading, I can shape different visual identities.
